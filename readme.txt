Every day a fresh draughts shot on your website.

== Description ==

Slagzet van de dag(Dutch for draughts shot of the day) is a widget that shows the daily draughts shots as featured on slagzet.com. With the navigation buttons under the shot you can see the solution.

The daily shots are provided by slagzet.com. No information about you or your visitors is sent to slagzet.com.

== Installation ==

1. Install the Plugin
1. Activate it
1. Drag the widget to your side bar
